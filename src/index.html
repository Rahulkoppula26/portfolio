About Me

Full stack Developer

Hello! I’m Koppula Rahul, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer based in Karimnagar, Telangana. I have a strong enthusiasm for designing and developing dynamic web applications using the MERN stack. My development philosophy revolves around merging thoughtful design with efficient backend logic to deliver smooth, modern user experiences. I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Vaageswari College of Engineering (JNTUH). Throughout my learning journey, I’ve developed full-stack projects like a YouTube Clone, a Shoppy Globe E-Commerce system with role-based authentication, and a Kanban Board featuring real-time drag-and-drop task management. Certified in Full Stack Web Development from Internshala Trainings and TCS iON Career Edge – Young Professional, I’ve cultivated a professional approach to teamwork, agile workflows, and continuous learning. I’m always driven by curiosity—experimenting with new tools, enhancing application performance, and building scalable solutions that make a difference. When I’m not coding, I explore new technologies, read about emerging trends, or brainstorm ideas for my next project.
